About lumo

lumo is a smart light dimmer at $30 with some seriously cool tech. Installs like a normal light dimmer, gives full capacitive control from the wall and away from the wall with an intuitive and elegant mobile-app.

Scenes

Group your lights to be controlled together. Perfect for movie nights, romantic evenings & much more!

Alerts

Light control based on triggered events. Perfect to wake up to & fall asleep with.
