About lumo

lumo is a $30 smart light dimmer with some seriously cool tech.The touch interface installs just like a normal light dimmer, giving unprecedented control from the wall. Control your lights away from the wall with an intuitive and elegant mobile-app, giving even more control from your pocket.

Scenes

Group your lights to be controlled together, save custom brightness configurations. Perfect for movie nights, romantic evenings, & much more!

Alerts

Customizable light control based on triggered events. Slowly turn on the lights to wake up to, or dim them to fall asleep with.

Energy

Track your energy usage over time, in real time. Learn how and where you can save energy. Help make a difference in the world.

Notifications

Get notifications if you've left your lights on, ask lumo to turn them off for you automatically or toggle them on the go.
