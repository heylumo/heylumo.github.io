The first affordable home automation solution, because a smart home is something everyone should have.

lumo is a $30 smart light dimmer. The touch interface installs just like a normal light dimmer, giving unprecedented control from the wall. Control your lights away from the wall with an intuitive and elegant mobile-app, giving even more control from your pocket.

Group your lights to be controlled together, save custom brightness configurations. Perfect for movie nights, romantic evenings, turning off the lights before sleep, & much more!

Track your energy usage over time, in real time. Learn how and where you can save energy. Help make a difference in the world.

Customizable alerts based on triggered events. Slowly turn on the lights to wake up to, or dim them to fall asleep with.

Get notifications if you've left your lights on, ask lumo to turn them off for you automatically or toggle them on the go.

lumo is developer friendly, that means you can write your own applications to integrate lumo with your other smart home devices.
